Intro & Motivation

Data: it's everywhere!

  • Wouldn't it be nice to have some application where I can see real data analysis?
  • And some graphics?

All this polls and data analysis hidden from the reader is a bad thing.

We would like you to have access to the important numbers that define if a data analysis is really successful.

Even More Spourious Correlation!

You choose which data to make a prediction relationship… and voil! Simple application, amazing results!

Statistics Involved

  • R2 is a measure of the models quality. Bigger is better!
[1] "GOOD! (R = 0.651079380758251 )"
  • The F statistic tells you whether the model is significant or insignificant.
[1] "SIGNIFICANT! (F statistic: 89.5671065364677 )"
  • We also show you the correlation between the sets of data you chose:
[1] 0.8069

If the correlation is close to 1, variables are positively linearly related. For -1, variables are negatively linearly. And for zero, it would indicates a weak linear relationship between the variables.

Conclusion

This app is just to elude how everyone without the knowledge of statistics can be mislead with erroneous interpretation of data analysis.
